About

Francis Maina was born and raised in the beautiful mountain side of Nanyuki, Kenya. Growing up, he was fascinated with rugby and this exposure led to him interacting with some of the wildest individuals. He is a first time author, a Televisions programs producer, a photographer, a car enthusiast and a car dealer. He graduated from the Kenya Institute of Mass Communication where he learnt to write Television scripts. He is a husband and a father of a beautiful baby girl. Now a part time writer, Mr. Frank and his wife call the city of Nairobi home.
